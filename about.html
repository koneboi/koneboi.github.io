About Me

Pharmacist and Research Scientist at PDNA and PMRTC with strong training in molecular biology, genomics, microbiology, and bioinformatics.

My research interests focus on pathogen genomic diversity, antimicrobial resistance (AMR), and infectious disease genomics. I am highly motivated, detail-oriented, and committed to pursuing doctoral training to develop independent research capacity in genomics and computational biology.

Research Interests

  • Pathogen genomics and genomic diversity
  • Antimicrobial resistance (AMR)
  • Molecular epidemiology of infectious diseases
  • Molecular surveillance for public health
  • Genomics and bioinformatics
