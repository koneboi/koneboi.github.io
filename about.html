About Me

I am a bioinformatics scientist focused on translating complex genomic signals into actionable science.

With experience spanning academic research, public health, and industry, I build reproducible pipelines, interactive dashboards, and predictive models that help research groups accelerate discoveries in human genomics, infectious diseases, and precision medicine.

My work has contributed to regional outbreak response in West Africa, continental-scale pathogen surveillance networks, and machine learning models for disease prediction.

Global Collaborations

I work with partners across Africa, Europe, and North America—from pathogen surveillance in Dakar to precision oncology collaborations in Europe.
